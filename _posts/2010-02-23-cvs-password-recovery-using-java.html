Have you forgotten your CVS password? If you have performed a cvs login on the machine in the past then you should have a .cvspass file stored in your home directory. This file contains an encrypted version of your password, and you can easily recover your password from it. Its contents will look something like this.

/1 :pserver:user@server:2401/cvs A:yZZ30 e</span></code>

The red text is the encrypted version of your password.

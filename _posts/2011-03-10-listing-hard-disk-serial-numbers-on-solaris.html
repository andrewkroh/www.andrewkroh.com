When a device has a name like /dev/dsk/c7t0d0 how do you know what physical hard disk this maps to in your Solaris system? Locating a physical hard disk by its device name can be achieved by having Solaris list the device serial numbers and then looking at the labels on the hard disks.

You can get Solaris to spit out the serial numbers by running the following command.

$ cfgadm -alv | grep SN
